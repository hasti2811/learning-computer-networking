ping - command used to test connectivity between your computer and another device (website/server) by sending
small packets of data and measuring how long it takes from them to travel back and forth.

you can ping either the domain name or the IP address (ping amazon.com/ping 206.253.89.151)

ping amazon.com:

hasti@DESKTOP-V3TMK7G:/mnt/c/Users/Hasti Amin/OneDrive/Desktop$ ping 206.253.89.151
